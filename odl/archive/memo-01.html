Memo 01 :: Diagram Omission Policy

DIA/OMIT/01

Public-facing diagrams shall omit deferred corridors pending continuity review. Omission is a publication rule only and does not alter continuity assignment, station persistence, corridor registry IDs, or transfer liabilities.

Where a station remains physically provisioned but publicly unlisted, the station shall be retained in internal ledgers with omitted public marker. Transfer capability continues where tagged as allowed in the station ledger.

Registry operators must reference the station ledger before route validation.
