Memo 02 :: Retained Station Encoding

LED/ENC/02

Omitted public stations shall remain in the ledger where deferred service potential is retained. For continuity extraction tasks, operators are instructed to filter station records by:

  • Public Listing = Omitted
  • Transfer Allowed = Yes

Read filtered records in listed order and extract initial letters from station names.

Appendix references for C-table remain uncataloged in this shelf.
