NTC-317

Transfer Protocol Amendment — Retained Corridors

Network Notice

Transfer eligibility at stations on suspended or retained lines is not automatically revoked. Where a station's platform remains physically provisioned, any authorised transfer allocation remains valid for continuity planning purposes.

This notice applies specifically to stations on lines at Amber Retention or above. Transfer allocations at Deactivated lines are subject to separate review.
