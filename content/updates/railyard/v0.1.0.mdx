We're excited to announce the first public release of **Railyard** - the all-in-one map and mod manager for Subway Builder.

Railyard is a free, open-source desktop app that makes it easy to discover, install, and manage community-made content for Subway Builder. No more digging through Discord threads or manually dragging files into folders - just browse, click, and play.

**Download:** https://subwaybuildermodded.com/railyard

_Railyard is still in beta, so please help us out by reporting any problems you have on the GitHub repository!_

## For Modders & Map Creators

Want your project in Railyard? Host your files on GitHub, fill out a submission form, and a maintainer will review it.

- Publishing a mod or map: https://subwaybuildermodded.com/railyard/docs/latest/developers/publishing-projects
- Publishing a map pack: https://subwaybuildermodded.com/railyard/docs/latest/developers/publishing-map-packs

And to everyone in the community who has been creating maps, building mods, testing early builds, and giving feedback — thank you. This project is for you! :heart:
