Route History

Line 9 provided cross-sector service between Vantage Cross in Sector A and Knoll End in Sector E, serving three intermediate stations. The line was valued for connecting sectors that would otherwise require transfers across two additional lines.

Following a network capacity review, Line 9 was designated for temporary suspension and placed on Amber Retention status. Amber Retention indicates the line remains administratively active and may be reactivated without a full service redesign.
