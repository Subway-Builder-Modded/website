Signal Codebook — Service Key 9

Alphabetic shift reference for Line 9 retained transmissions

SCB/KEY-9
Line 9 transmissions are encoded by shifting each letter 9 positions forward in the alphabet. To decode a transmission: find the encoded letter in the left column and read the original letter in the right column. Dashes are separators and are not decoded.
