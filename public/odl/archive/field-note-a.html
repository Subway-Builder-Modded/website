Field Note A :: Platform Signage Audit

FLD/PLT/A

Signage visible from public mezzanine shall terminate at listed service only. Deferred platforms may remain physically signed at track level where line continuity and maintenance movement require consistency.

Platform D references are retained in station backhouse schedules and continuity notices, despite omission from public boards.

Auditors should not infer cancellation from omission.

Cross-check: Notice 194 and station ledger.
