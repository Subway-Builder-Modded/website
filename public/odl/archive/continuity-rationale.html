Continuity Rationale

POL/CONT/R

Deferred does not indicate cancellation. Deferred indicates retained service potential outside public listing. This status permits continuity accounting, reserve planning, right-of-way retention, and transfer scenario testing without publication obligations.

The Office of Deferred Lines maintains records for contingencies where reopening, service diversion, or non-public transfers may be reactivated without complete redesign.

Administrative retention minimizes downstream planning debt and preserves interoperability with existing sector allocations.

Reference set: Memo 01, Notice 311, Corridor Registry.
