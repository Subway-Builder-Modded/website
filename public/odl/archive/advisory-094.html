ADV-094

Line 9 — Service Suspension and Continuity Retention Notice

Active Advisory
Issuing officeOffice of Deferred Lines
Line affectedLine 9 (Vantage Cross — Knoll End)
EffectPublic service suspended. Administrative retention active.
Retention levelAmber
Transfer noticeNTC-317

1. Summary

Line 9 has been removed from the public-facing network map following a capacity review. This advisory confirms that the line has not been formally cancelled. It has been placed on Amber Retention status and remains documented within ODL's internal continuity registry pending resolution of the routing review.

2. Affected Stations

Five stations are affected by this suspension: Vantage Cross, Ember Gate, Lunar Street, Orchard Bridge, and Knoll End. All station records, platform assignments, and transfer allocations remain active in the internal registry. Transfer eligibility is preserved under NTC-317.

For full station detail see the Line 9 continuity file →

3. Public Communication

Public timetables have been updated to remove Line 9 references. Signage at terminal stations has been revised. Intermediate platform signage remains subject to review. No formal cancellation announcement has been authorised.

4. Encoded Transmissions

All retained continuity transmissions for Line 9 are encoded using the Service Key 9 encoding cipher. This cipher is internal and does not appear in publicly distributed documents.

Appendix A — Station Access Designation

The retained station registry for Line 9 is maintained under the following internal designation. This designation does not appear in publicly distributed documents.
